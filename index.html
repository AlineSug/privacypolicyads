Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Our mobile applications the App is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require registration, login, or personal data to function. We do not collect, store, process, or share:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Photos, media, files
  • Health or fitness data
  • Payment or financial information

All workout timers and counters run entirely on your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising

The App displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob.

We have configured AdMob to request and display non-personalized ads (NPA), meaning:

  • No personalized ad targeting
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No user profiling

AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID) and general technical information required to deliver ads. This data is processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

Learn more:
Google AdMob Privacy Policy — https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

The App does not store user data on external servers. Any information generated within the App remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall it.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there are no security risks related to stored user information.

6. Your Privacy Choices

Users may restrict ad tracking through device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset/Disable Ad ID

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page with a revised date.
